    Welcome to DALPS

    The DALPS or sh data analytics lifecycle Process & Standardization portal is the one stop shop to know about the best practices, processes, standards, guidelines and lifecycle stages covering 3 verticals ie Data, ERP and AI. Thus this portal will help projects, products and platforms working on Data and Analytics space around Data Engineering, Data Visualization, AI Engineering, ML techniques & ERP analytics with the technology agnostic principles and also providing required resources (or links) to relevant tools and technologies.
